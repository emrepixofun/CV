Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Nöbetçi Eczane" handles user information.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

The application does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app may process limited non-personal data, including:

- Selected city/location (used to display nearby pharmacies)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address
- Usage data
- Advertising identifiers

This data is used strictly for providing the service and improving app performance.

---

### 2. Health-Related Data

The application provides health-related information (pharmacy availability).  

However:
- The app does not collect or store any personal health data
- No medical records, diagnosis, or user health information is collected

---

### 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services:

Google AdMob
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- Ad interaction data

More info: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

External APIs
- City-based pharmacy data requests
- Request metadata (IP, device info)

---

### 4. Data Storage and Retention

- Data is stored temporarily in Firebase Realtime Database
- Cached data is kept for a limited time (typically less than 24 hours)
- No personal data is permanently stored
