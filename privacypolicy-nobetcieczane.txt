Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Nöbetçi Eczane" handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

Device information (model, OS version)

IP address

Usage data

Advertising identifiers

These data are collected solely for analytics and advertising purposes.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process user data such as:

Advertising ID

Device information

Interaction with ads

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

External APIs

The App fetches pharmacy data using third-party APIs. These APIs may process:

Selected city information

Request metadata (IP, device)

No personal data is sent by the App.

3. Data Storage and Retention

The App uses Firebase Realtime Database to temporarily store data for performance optimization.

Data is cached for a limited time (typically within the same day)

Data is automatically refreshed and overwritten

No personal user data is stored permanently

4. Location Information

The App does not access precise user location.

Users manually select their city within the App.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed through the App. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically.
